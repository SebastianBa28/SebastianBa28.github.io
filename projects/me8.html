ME 8 — Gimbal Tracking System

Project Overview
Designed and fabricated a camera platform incorporating precision motors to control the pan and tilt positions of the camera. Developed a vision system to identify objects of interest and follow them to remain at the center of the frame at all times.

System Overview

Mechanical Design

Designed L-bracket attachments to connect two stepper motors and hold a camera. Parts were manufactured using a 3D Printer and Laser Cutter. The entire assembly was modeled in OnShape and integrated on a Raspberry Pi microcontroller.

Detection & Following Routine

Implemented Cascade Classifiers using OpenCV to detect human faces. Developed a custom vision detection routine to identify tennis balls using binary masking and Gaussian blurring. A PID controller drives the camera to keep the detected object centered in frame.
