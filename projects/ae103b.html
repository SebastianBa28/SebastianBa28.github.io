Ae 103b — Drone Trajectory Planning

Project Overview
Developed a drone navigation system featuring minimum-snap trajectory generation formulated as a quadratic programming optimization problem. Derived 6-DOF nonlinear vehicle dynamics and implemented a PID controller for attitude and position stabilization. Integrated the full system in Simulink, validating waypoint tracking, disturbance rejection under wind gusts, and tradeoffs between trajectory aggressiveness and tracking error.
