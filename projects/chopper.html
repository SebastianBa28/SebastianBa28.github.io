CHOPPER Rotor Interaction
Characterization

Project Overview
Led experimental study of rotor aerodynamics for CHOPPER, the next-generation Mars Helicopter. Developed an underwater experiment designed to preserve flow properties under Martian flight regimes. This allowed us to characterize the effects of rotor-rotor interactions on thrust generation characteristics and flight dynamics, identifying up to 25% thrust reduction in adverse flight conditions.
