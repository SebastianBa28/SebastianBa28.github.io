About Me

A bit about myself

My name is Sebastian — I am a fourth-year Mechanical Engineering student at Caltech with a minor in Controls and Dynamical Systems. Through my coursework and work experience I have developed a solid foundation in control theory, mathematical optimization, and robotic planning. I am particularly interested in applying these skills to the design and implementation of guidance, navigation, and control (GNC) systems in aerospace.

Outside of engineering I enjoy cycling through scenic landscapes, theatre and modern dance, and reading science fiction — particularly Isaac Asimov and Philip K. Dick. I am fluent in both English and Spanish.
