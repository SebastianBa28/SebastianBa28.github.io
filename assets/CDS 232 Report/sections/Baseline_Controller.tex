\section{\textbf{Baseline Controller}}

\noindent Given a desired state and control input along the trajectory $(x_{ref}, u_{ref})$, define the errors
\[ e = x - x_{ref}\]
\[ e_u = u - u_{ref} \]

\noindent such that the error dynamics are
\[ \begin{aligned}
    \dot{e} &= \dot{x} - \dot{x}_{ref} \\
    &= f(x, u) - f(x_{ref}, u_{ref}) \\
    &= f(x_{ref} + e, u_{ref} + e_u) - f(x_{ref}, u_{ref})
\end{aligned} \]

\noindent Let the function
\[ g(e, e_u) = f(x_{ref} + e, u_{ref} + e_u) - f(x_{ref}, u_{ref}) \]

\noindent Linearize the error dynamics about $(0,0)$
\[ g(e, e_u) \approx g(0,0) + \frac{\partial g}{\partial \tilde x} \Bigg|_{0,0} e + \frac{\partial g}{\partial e_u} \Bigg|_{0,0} e_u \]

\noindent Note $g(0,0) = 0$. By the chain rule we have
\[ \frac{\partial g}{\partial \tilde x} \Bigg|_{0,0} = \frac{\partial f}{\partial x} \Bigg|_{x_{ref}, u_{ref}}
\quad
\frac{\partial g}{\partial e_u} \Bigg|_{0,0} = \frac{\partial f}{\partial u} \Bigg|_{x_{ref}, u_{ref}} \]

\noindent Thus, we are left with the linear system
\[ \dot{e} = Ae + B e_u\]

\noindent The explicit form of $A$ and $B$ are presented in the index. \\

\noindent Finally, consider the control law of the form $u = u_{ref} - ke$ where the gain $k$ is computed as LQR.
