\section{\textbf{Future Work}}

\noindent We have shown the ability of constructing barrier functions to render a safe set in the presence of a trajectory tracking controller that appears to violate safety conditions. However, the by blending these two control inputs, we give up the ability to follow the trajectory "on-time". Therefore, a future research direction of this project is to explore methods for online planning in the presence of safety conditions. For instance, solving an MPC formulation with a CBF layer. Futhermore, during the derivation above, it was assumed that the obstacle was at a fixed location. Thus, another interesting research direction is to formulate CBFs for obstacle avoidance of moving targets.
