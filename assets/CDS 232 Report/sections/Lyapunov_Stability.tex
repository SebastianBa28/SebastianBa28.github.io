\section{\textbf{Lyapunov Stability}}

\noindent Consider the candidate Lyapunov function

$$V(x) = x^\top P x$$

\noindent where $P = P^\top \succ 0$ satisfying the CTLE

$$A^\top P + PA = -Q$$

\noindent From the conditions for stability we have

$$\lambda_{min}(P) \|x\|^2 \leq V(x) \leq \lambda_{max}(P) \|x\|^2$$

\noindent We verify the second condition we have

$$\begin{aligned}
    \dot{V}(x) &= \dot{x}^\top P x + x^\top P \dot{x} \\
               &= x^\top A^\top P x + x^\top P Ax \\
               &= x^\top (A^\top P + PA) x \\
               &= -x^\top Q x
\end{aligned}$$

\noindent Thus, we have

$$\dot{V}(x) \leq -\lambda_{min}(Q) \|x\|^2$$

\noindent In this case, taking $Q = I_6$ we compute $P$ for the closed loop system, which yields the eigenvalues
$$\lambda(P) = \begin{bmatrix}
    0.1129 \\
    0.2701 \\
    1.1506 \\
    1.4446 \\
    6.7640 \\
    7.7801
\end{bmatrix}$$

\noindent Thus, we have

$$0.1129 \; \|x\|^2 \leq V(x) \leq 7.7801 \; \|x\|^2$$

$$\dot{V}(x) \leq - \| x \|^2$$
