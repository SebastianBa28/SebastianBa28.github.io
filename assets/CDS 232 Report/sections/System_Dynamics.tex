\section{\textbf{System Dynamics}}

\noindent For a drone of mass $m$ and thrust inputs $u_1,u_2$, the translational dynamics expressed in the inertial frame are given by
\[ m\ddot{x} = - (u_1 + u_2) \sin \theta \]
\[ m\ddot{y} = (u_1 + u_2) \cos \theta - mg\]

\noindent The rotational dynamics are given by
\[ I\ddot{\theta} = r(u_1 - u_2) \]

\noindent where $I$ is the moment and $r$ is the distance from the motors to the CoM.

\noindent Considering the state vector
\[ x = \begin{bmatrix}
    x & \dot{x} & y & \dot{y} & \theta & \dot{\theta}
\end{bmatrix}\]

\noindent We write the system dynamics as follows
\[ \dot{x} = \begin{bmatrix}
    \dot{x} \\
    -(u_1 + u_2) \sin\theta / m \\
    \dot{y} \\
    (u_1 + u_2) \cos\theta / m - g \\
    \dot{\theta} \\
    r(u_1 - u_2) / I
\end{bmatrix} \]
